About the harbour

Built by fishermen, run for fishermen.

For generations, this stretch of the Coastal Road has been where boats land their catch, auctions set the day's price, and cold storage keeps it fresh for the journey onward. Today the harbour combines that working tradition with modern docking, auction and transport facilities.
