About our harbour

Modern facilities, built on a working tradition.

Our fishing harbour provides modern facilities for fishermen including boat docking, fish auction, cold storage, and transportation services — all run from a single stretch of the Coastal Road that has served the fleet for decades.

Harbour history

From a landing point to a working port

Early years

A simple landing point

Fishermen first beached their boats along this stretch of coast, selling the day's catch directly on the sand to traders from nearby villages.

Growth phase

The first jetty and auction yard

As the fleet grew, a permanent jetty and an open auction yard were built, giving boats a proper place to dock and fixing a transparent way to set the day's prices.

Modernisation

Cold storage and transport links

Cold storage units and dedicated transport tie-ups were added, letting the catch travel further while staying fresh, and opening up new markets for local fishermen.

Today

A fully serviced harbour

The harbour now supports over a hundred registered boats with docking, auction, storage and transport under one administration — and this portal, so the office is reachable online too.

Facilities

What's available on-site

Docking

Boat Docking Bays

Marked bays sized for everything from single-fishermen craft to larger trawlers, with clear turnaround scheduling.

Auction

Fish Auction Yard

A transparent daily auction where the catch is weighed, graded and sold at a price visible to every fisherman present.

Storage

Cold Storage

Round-the-clock cold storage so the day's catch stays fresh until it's sold, packed or sent onward.

Transport

Transportation Services

On-site tie-ups with transport operators to move fish quickly to markets beyond the harbour.

Welfare

Fishermen's Facilities

Rest areas, drinking water and basic amenities for fishermen and crew between trips.

Safety

Harbour Office & First Aid

A staffed office handling registrations and disputes, with basic first-aid support on hand.

Infrastructure

Built to keep the harbour running

Jetty & Breakwater

A reinforced jetty and breakwater protect docked boats from rough water during the monsoon season.

Power & Cold-chain

A dedicated power supply keeps cold storage and ice plants running through peak landing hours.

Approach Road

A widened approach road off Coastal Road handles transport trucks without disrupting fishermen traffic.
